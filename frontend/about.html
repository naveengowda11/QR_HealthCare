QR Health Access System

QR Health Access System is a hospital-driven digital health platform designed to solve one of the most critical problems in emergency healthcare: the lack of verified patient medical information at the point of care. In real-world emergencies, patients are often unconscious or unable to communicate their medical history, leading doctors to make decisions with incomplete data.

This system ensures that patient information is entered and verified only by hospitals, eliminating errors caused by self-reported data. Each patient is issued a unique QR code that provides limited but life-saving information during emergencies, while full medical records remain securely accessible only to authorized doctors.

By combining role-based access control, secure data handling, and QR-based retrieval, the platform bridges the gap between emergency response and informed clinical decision-making. The solution is designed as a centralized national platform, scalable across hospitals, and adaptable for future integration with government health systems and AI-driven diagnostics.
